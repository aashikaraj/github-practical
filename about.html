My name is Aashika Raj, and Tech Ignited My Passion

Previously, I was deeply engrossed in my possessions. I collected various items that I thought brought me joy, but they only cluttered my life, hindering true appreciation.

My situation was challenging. I felt disconnected from my surroundings, struggling to find my true calling.

My Journey to Transformation

I reached a pivotal moment when I decided to simplify. I let go of unnecessary belongings, embracing a minimalist lifestyle and freeing myself from excess baggage.

The path wasn't easy, but amidst the changes, I found clarity and purpose.

Embracing Simplicity

Now, simplicity defines my existence. I'm liberated from unnecessary stress, able to fully immerse myself in my studies and pursuits.

Recognizing the profound impact it had on me, I felt compelled to share my journey and inspire others to discover the joys of simplicity. Thus, this platform was born!
